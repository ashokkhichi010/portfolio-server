#### Problem

When I first joined a library, I had to pay for 30 days even though I only needed 15–20 days. I also observed that most libraries still use manual systems like paper or Excel.

#### What I Built

* Subscription management system
* Seat allocation system
* Admin workflows for library operations
* Multi-tenant SaaS architecture

#### What I Learned

* Product-market fit is critical
* Trust matters more than features
* Real users behave differently than expected
* Building without research is a major mistake

Library owners prefer manual systems due to trust issues and simplicity. This taught me that **adoption is a bigger challenge than building**.
